Details

CV. Jogja Smart Indotama (JSI) is an institution/company that domiciled in Yogyakarta and operates in the field of providing training and services consultant for Human Resources in all companies in Indonesia and abroad. The purpose of establishing this company is to bridging HR training needs with experts from the world academics and various practitioners, so that links and matches are formed between both the industrial world and the academic world as well as practitioners. The initial stages of registration activities carried out by registrants are still ongoing not yet using an integrated information system, currently registrants must fill out the registration form provided by the agency and registrant send back the completed form via e-mail to the relevant party. Therefore, a complete system is needed to document training activities become better and easier. The system will also save client and training participant data which will later be useful in carrying out management. CV Training Management System Development. Jogja Smart Indotama uses the Waterfall method.

Features:

  • Login: Users can log in to the application by entering valid credentials. Upon successful login, users will be directed to the main dashboard.
  • Register: Users can create a new account by providing the required information. The registration process is straightforward and easy to follow.
  • Log Out: Users can easily log out of their account at any time to end their session.
  • Master data Contains important data on the system. (User, category, company, client)
  • Report: Reports containing training and financial data.
