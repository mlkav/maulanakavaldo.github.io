Details

In this Virtual Internship, I will be introduced to the role of a Data Engineer at Bank BTPN Syariah. I have the opportunity to understand the responsibilities and master the skills needed in the sharia banking industry. I also learned to use skills such as SQL, T-SQL, and data visualization tools such as Tableau, which are an important part of developing IT services. Apart from that, I also learned how to use Microsoft SQL Server SSIS to integrate and manage data efficiently. Through the practical projects and tasks presented, I was able to produce solutions that were relevant to user needs and gained valuable experience in the context of the Islamic banking industry.
